How to Play

  • Make your choices by reading and clicking the links on the chatbox.
  • Navigate through the map by opening it on the bottom right box, you can leave your location at anytime you want.
  • Keep track of what you have on you with your inventory, the upper right box.
  • The game has multiple endings, if you got a bad one, maybe you forgot to look somewhere.
  • Don't forget to check out the settings and choose the best options for your playstyle.
  • Have fun!
